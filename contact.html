Frequently Asked Questions

Urban gardening is the practice of cultivating, processing, and distributing food in or around urban areas. It includes various methods such as container gardening, rooftop gardening, and community gardens.

Some of the best plants for urban gardening include herbs (basil, parsley, mint), vegetables (tomatoes, peppers, lettuce), and flowers (marigolds, petunias, zinnias).

To start a rooftop garden, assess the roof's structure for load capacity, ensure proper drainage, use lightweight soil, and select appropriate plants. Additionally, consider installing windbreaks and irrigation systems.
